ZESTY Orange Mocha

Chocolate and bananas are a classic smoothie combination.

And for good reason—it's a not-too-sweet, yet totally indulgent mix that can satisfy just about anyone's sweet tooth.

Ingredients

  • 1 frozen orange, cut into chunks
  • 1 scoop chocolate protein powder
  • 2 dates, pitted
  • ⅔ cup coconut water
  • ⅓ cup tart cherry juice
