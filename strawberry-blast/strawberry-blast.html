Strawberry BLAST!

Strawberry and chocolate make for the perfect combination of tart and sweet in this easy-to-make smoothie recipe.

Sneak a scoop or two of your favorite protein powder into the mix for a muscle-building boost.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups almond or coconut milk
  • 1 10-oz bag of frozen strawberries
  • 1 Tbsp cacao powder
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract (optional)
  • 3 Tbsp cacao nibs
  • 5-6 ice cubes
  • 1 scoop favorite protein powder
